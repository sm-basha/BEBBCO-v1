About Us

B E B B C O

BEBBCO Group is known as diversified businesses in this region for last 36 years. Started with the establishment of BHARAT ENGG. & BODY BUILDING CO. PVT. LTD. in the Year 1983, The group headed by our Chairman Shree Gajanand Bhalotia .

BEBBCO specializes in fabrication of "Automobile Body". We build customized body for automobile depending on the purpose for the vehicle.

At BEBBCO we have fabricated the following Automobile bodies on TATA, EICHER, Ashok Leyland, BHARAT BENZ, URAL Chassis ranging from 2600 WB to 6200 WB for domestic as well as for export market.


“We make anything on Wheels”

Our consistent effort is to earn & retain the customer’s confidence and goodwill which will inspire us to proactively work in providing products which exceeds customer expectation in terms of quality and price.
To ensure the same we intend enhance our infrastructure from time to time keeping our workshop abreast with latest technology & machinery with skilled workforce and stringent quality control.

Our endeavour is to provide improved customized design & fabrication of motor vehicles, ranging from normal cargo transportation, passenger vehicles to specialized “service on wheels” vehicles such as "Caravan, Mobile - Office, Medical unit, Marketing campaign, Catering van, Kaushalyan (Special purpose training vehicle) and so on.
